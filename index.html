About Core

MBCF currently owns 3 Nextseq and 3 Miseq machines along with Sanger, Neoprep and other robotic equipment to expedite the process of data generation. We have end to end support of library preparation, sequencing, bioinformatics analysis. We also undertake project of custom nature that requires development of algorithms, analysis pipelines and visualization of results as publication ready plots.
